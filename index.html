Our History and Mission

The history of our hair salon is one of friendship, passion, and expertise. Founded by Pria and Garry, two close friends with a shared love for hairdressing, our salon has become a go-to destination for quality hair services. Pria, who earned her certificate as a colorist, has a keen eye for color and is skilled in creating unique and personalized hair color for her clients. Garry, on the other hand, is a talented hairdresser with years of experience in cutting, styling, and hair care. Together, they have created a welcoming and professional space where clients can come and feel their best. With a focus on quality, creativity, and client satisfaction, our salon has built a reputation for excellence over the years.
